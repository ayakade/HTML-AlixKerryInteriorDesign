Services

Alix Kerry Interior Design continually strives to create homes that boast a refined elegance and balance. My ethos is to allow design to forever maintain relevance. I introduce this concept through classical design, infused with clean modern edges and quality materials.

Initial Consultation

Contact me for a consultation. I would love to sit down with you face-to-face and get a better sense of the project scope and direction.

Project Proposal

Following the initial consultation, I provide you with an estimate of your project and the stages in which it will progress. This detailed estimate outlines the scope, fee and timeline proposed.

Construction

At this exciting time, I supervise our projects ‘on the ground’ to ensure every aspect of the design is moving forward seamlessly. Working closely with the contractor and installers I will be on site at regular intervals, assisting with coordination and ensuring accuracy to the design intent.

Finishing Touches

I work alongside you to curate your space with furnishings and accessories, an important stage that compliments the interior architecture of your home. As a full service Interior Design firm, I have the ability to source custom made furniture and fabrics to create that final ‘wow factor’. This stage is often the most rewarding – transforming your house to a home.
